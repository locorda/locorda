## Philosophy

**Offline-first**: Apps should work perfectly offline, syncing when connected.

**BOS - Bring your Own Storage**: Users choose where their data is stored - Solid Pods, Google Drive, or their own infrastructure. Be the boss of your data.

**No backend required**: Sync happens directly between devices and your chosen BOS storage.

**Standards-based**: Built on proven web standards (RDF, Linked Data) for interoperability and longevity.
